
# Flat roofed house experiment 🏠


> An experiment to record and analyze the temperature change inside a flat roof home model over time and compare it's thermal behaviour with houses with other shapes.

---

## Our method
 - A temperature sensor recorded the internal temperature every 5 seconds
 - Data was collected as the flat roofed model home was exposed to a heating element
 - Temperatures were then recorded and then plotted onto a scatter graph against time and temperature
 - A linear trendline was also added to calculate the heating rate/cooling rate

---
